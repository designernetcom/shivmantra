Storage Silo

we design and manufacture storage silo used in various industries as per customer requirement Storage silos are cylindrical structures, typically 10 to 90 ft (3 to 27 m) in diameter and 30 to 275 ft (10 to 90 m) in height. They can be made of many materials such as wood staves, concrete staves, cast concrete, and steel panels. Silos can be unloaded into rail cars, trucks or conveyors.

Type of Silo :
  • Cement Silo
  • Food Grain Silo
  • Battery Plant Silo
